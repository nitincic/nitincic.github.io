About Me

Privacy-preserving ML • AI privacy • Fairness & Compliance

I’m Nitin Agrawal, a Privacy Engineer at Snap. I work on privacy validation, building an internal line of defense through vulnerability detection and proactive privacy mitigation. I also work on privacy, safety, fairness, and trustworthy AI, shaping validation, governance, and mitigations for ML systems. My work spans privacy risk measurement, auditing, privacy-enhancing technologies (crypto, federated learning), and systems that maximize business utility while staying compliant and safeguarding users and customer trust.
